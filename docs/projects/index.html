Projects

Research and development projects across computational neuroscience, connectomics, and embodied robotics.

Research & Reference Figures

Hemispheric-Specific Wilson-Cowan Model

Modeling resting-state functional connectivity with asymmetric intra- and inter-hemispheric coupling.

Adaptive Networks with Chaotic Units

Studying how connection density shapes synchronization, integration, and modular organization in adaptive dynamical networks.

Connectome-Driven Embodied Robotics

Building robotic control architectures from biological connectomes to study autonomy, proprioception, and sensorimotor feedback.

Drosophila Leg and Wing Motor Connectomics

Connectomics of Drosophila leg and wing motor circuits, including identified motor neurons, front-leg muscles, and the premotor-motor connectivity matrix.

Robotics Prototypes & Evolution

Hexapod Joystick Control - Natural Motions

Silent demo of joystick-controlled hexapod locomotion with natural motion primitives.

Hexapod Robot Gaits (18-DoF)

Tripod, ripple, and wave gait experiments on an 18-servo hexapod platform.

Single Leg Servo Calibration

Offset tuning and motion calibration for early hexapod leg control.

Animatronic Head + Torso

Eyes, neck, shoulders, and elbows integrated into a PS2-controlled animatronic prototype.

Dual-Eye Saccades and Blink

Eye actuation prototype for saccadic motion and coordinated blinking.

Mecanum Omni-Directional Robot

Mecanum-wheel mobile base with PS2 joystick control and ultrasonic sensing.

Two-Wheel Obstacle Avoidance

Braitenberg-inspired controller using ultrasonic and light sensing.

Strandbeest-Style Walker

Theo Jansen-inspired walking mechanism prototype driven by continuous-rotation servos.
